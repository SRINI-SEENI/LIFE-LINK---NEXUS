# Life Link Nexus

### *Every second, let's connect to save many lives* 

A comprehensive healthcare platform designed to bridge the gap between patients, healthcare providers, and life-saving resources. Built with **Real-time Database** for instant data synchronization and **Machine Learning models** for intelligent health predictions.

---

##  What Makes Us Special

 **Blood & Organ Donation Network** — Seamlessly connect donors with recipients in real-time. Our platform maintains a live database of available donors, making critical matches faster than ever.

 **AI-Powered Health Predictions** — Using trained ML models, users can assess their risk for **Diabetes** and **Heart Disease** instantly. Get personalized insights based on medical parameters.

 **Doctor Appointments** — Book consultations with verified healthcare providers. Real-time slot availability and instant confirmation.

 **E-Pharmacy** — Order medicines online with prescription management. Admins can register pharmacies and manage inventory seamlessly.

 **E-FIR System** — Emergency incident reporting for quick response and documentation.

## Machine Learning Integration

Our platform includes **trained ML models** for health risk assessment:

- **Diabetes Prediction Model** — Analyzes glucose levels, BMI, blood pressure, and other factors
- **Heart Disease Model** — Evaluates cholesterol, heart rate, age, and lifestyle parameters

Both models are trained using real medical datasets and provide instant risk scores with personalized recommendations.
